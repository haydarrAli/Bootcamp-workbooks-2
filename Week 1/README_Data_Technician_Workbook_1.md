# Data Technician Workbook 1

## Overview
In this workbook, I completed a range of core data tasks using Excel and business datasets. I worked on organising and analysing data, using formulas, tables, and pivot tables to summarise information and identify patterns. I also categorised data using the SWITCH function and created visualisations in Excel to present findings more clearly. Alongside the practical tasks, I covered key data protection and legal principles, including GDPR and the responsible handling of customer data. I also explored sales, churn, and revenue analysis, and practised presenting insights clearly for senior leaders.

## Topics Covered
- Data protection and GDPR
- Excel tables, sorting, and formulas
- Pivot tables
- Sales data analysis
- Data categorisation using SWITCH
- Data visualisation in Excel
- Churn and revenue analysis
- Presenting findings to senior leaders

## Skills Demonstrated
- Organising and cleaning structured datasets
- Using Excel functions such as SUM, AVERAGE, and SWITCH
- Building pivot tables to summarise data
- Interpreting sales patterns and customer trends
- Creating charts and visual outputs in Excel
- Communicating findings in a business context
- Applying data protection principles when handling customer data

## Workbook Highlights
- Converted raw sales data into structured Excel tables
- Sorted and analysed retail sales data
- Calculated commission totals and averages using formulas
- Built pivot tables from bike sales and product sales datasets
- Categorised sales volumes into high, medium, and low groups
- Identified patterns in customer churn and revenue impact
- Prepared responses on how to present analysis to board-level stakeholders
